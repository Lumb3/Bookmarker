YT Bookmarks

Save and manage your favorite YouTube moments

Not on a YouTube Video Page

This extension works on YouTube video pages. Please navigate to a video first.

How to use:

  1. Open any YouTube video
  2. Click the purple + button on the video player to bookmark
  3. Click the extension icon to view and manage your bookmarks
  4. Access your bookmark history in the History tab
